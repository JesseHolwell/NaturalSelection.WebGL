A virtual fishtank I built using Unity and C#, running on WebGL.

My goals were to: simulate biology and evolution to increase my understanding of the mechanics of evolution, and to build a program using Unity, to improve my programming skills.

How it works:

Large fish are males, and skinny fish are females.

Fish will grow up and age, seeking food when their energy levels get too low.

If their energy drops too low, or they get old enough, they will die.

Once they are old enough they will become fertile and occasionally seek a mate.

After mating, females become pregnant and will soon lay an egg,

The egg will will hatch a new baby fish.

The baby fish contains a combination of genes from both parents, sometimes with slight mutations

These mutations can be for better or worse, which enables better fish to eat more, and reproduce more

which furthers their genetic line.
